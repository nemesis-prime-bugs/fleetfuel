# FleetFuel — Product brief

## One-liner
Track fuel, trips, and costs for personal vehicles or small fleets—plus receipts—then see clean monthly charts.

## Target users
- Private individuals tracking one or more cars
- Small companies tracking a small fleet

## Core workflows
1) Signup/login (email + password)
2) Create account profile (personal/company)
3) Add vehicles
4) Log fill-ups (+ receipt photo)
5) Log trips (optionally pick driver)
6) Monthly charts + export

## Reporting (MVP)
- Monthly total fuel volume
- Monthly total spend
- Consumption over time (per vehicle)
- Cost per distance (optional)

## Constraints
- 0€ monthly recurring cost (free tiers OK)
- Receipt photos stored locally for now
